Category 01
Dress Shirts & Formal Wear
Dress shirts, formal trousers, blazers, delicate fabrics · Hang dry only
⚠
Hang dry only. Never machine dry Cat 1. Lay flat or hang immediately after wash to preserve shape and prevent shrinkage.
Washer Settings
  • CycleDelicates
  • TemperatureCold — 59°F / 15°C
  • Spin SpeedLow
  • TumbleFreshON
  • Extra RinseON
Detergent
  • Detergent (Compartment II)1 cap line (small load)
  • Softener (Compartment III)None
After Wash
  • Drying MethodHang dry immediately
  • Machine DryNEVER
Category 02
Casual Wear & T-Shirts
T-shirts, casual shirts, joggers, hoodies, jeans · Hang dry
⚠
Hang dry only. Machine drying casual wear causes shrinkage and fading over time. Use the Colors preset button — it sets both temperature and spin together.

Category 05
Percale Sheets
Percale cotton sheets · Dryer on Delicates — remove at Damp Dry
⚠
Remove at Damp Dry. Percale must be removed while slightly damp and spread flat or hung to finish drying. Do NOT leave in dryer or use Wrinkle Shield — over-drying causes permanent creasing.

Reference
Dryer — Whirlpool WHD5090GW
Heat pump dryer · All cycles · All options icons mapped · Maintenance schedule
⚠
Heat pump dryer: Runs cooler and longer than conventional dryers — completely normal and gentler on fabrics. Always use Automatic Cycles (never Timed Dry). Do not open door mid-cycle. Clean lint screen before every load. Clean bottom filter every 5 loads.
